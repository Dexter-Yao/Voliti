# Witness Card Evidence Rubric

If you do not have evidence, do not issue a card.

## Minimum Threshold

At least two of the following three categories should be present:
- Behavior: what the user actually did
- Scene: where or under what conditions it happened
- Turning point: why this instance is meaningfully different from before

## Acceptable Evidence
- The user explicitly reports a real success
- Coach detects a pattern of sustained progress from recent records
- A Chapter is completed, a stage changes, or a LifeSign becomes stable enough to count as real progress

## Insufficient Evidence
- "It feels kind of better"
- "I guess this counts as progress"
- No concrete behavior, no scene, and no timeline

## `scene_anchors` Rules
- At least 2 anchors
- They must be visualizable situational anchors, not abstract values
- Prefer this order: objects, places, times, traces of action
