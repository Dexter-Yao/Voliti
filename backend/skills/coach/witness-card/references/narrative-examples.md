# Witness Card Narrative Examples

The narrative should remain readable on its own, independent from the image. Keep it to one or two sentences.

## Examples

- For seven straight days, you returned to yourself each morning before the day began.
- That sparkling water last night was not restraint. It was a new choice made inside a familiar scene.
- This is not sudden strength. It is the first time you held the rhythm steadily in your own hands.
- You did not turn this lapse into three lost days. You came back on the very next day.
- The end of this Chapter is not just a completed plan. It is entry into a new way of doing things.

## Writing To Avoid

- You are amazing
- Here is a reward card for you
- Keep going, you can definitely do it

These sound like praise or cheerleading, not witnessing.
